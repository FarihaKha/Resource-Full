Because We Care

About Us

We are a group of girls who came together through the program, Kode with Klossy. Living in NYC as women of color, we understand that there is a lack of resources to help people of color in the "concrete jungle where dreams are made of." So, we created this website so that people like us living in New York could easily access resources. This website compiles all of the essential resources.
