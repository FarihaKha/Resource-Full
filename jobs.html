Job Websites

Now that you have the tools for getting a job, let's look for job placements!

Jopwell came to life on the trading floor of Goldman Sachs. The founders noticed a gap "between the desire to recruit and hire underrepresented talent and the available solutions to reach communities of color at scale". They knew there had to be a better way to connect diverse top talent with employers, so they built Topwell. Jopwell represents and advances careers for Black, Latinx, and Native American students & professionals!

People of Color Careers is a website that allows users to create free accounts in order to create a place for Professionals of Color to be their authentic selves and and meet hiring managers & recruiters. This hiring website was made with Black professionals and other People of Color in mind. It is an all-in-one spot for Professionals and Employers to find one another.

Diversity.com has been a leader in the diversity market since 2000, a true pioneer in the job diversity search. It features job postings from some of the most diverse employers across a wide variety of industries. It is best for finding employers that hire black and minority talent at all levels.

Professional Diversity Network pairs job seekers with employers employers who are serious about building a diverse workforce to meet the needs of an increasingly diverse customer base.
